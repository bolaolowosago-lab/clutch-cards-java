GAME NAME: Clutch Cards

This is a 2 player card game where the goal is to win 10 rounds before the other player. It’s mostly about not selling your hand and knowing when to play your stronger cards.

SETUP:
We use one standard 52 card deck. Each player gets 5 cards. Player 1 goes first in the first round.

HOW A ROUND STARTS:
At the start of each round, one card is flipped from the deck into the middle to start the pile.

HOW TURNS WORK:
Players take turns playing one card at a time.

You can play a card if:

* It has the same suit as the card on the pile, OR
* It has a higher rank than the card on the pile

SPECIAL RULE:
If a player plays an Ace, the pile resets. That means the next player can play any card they want.

If a player has no valid card they can play, they lose the round immediately.

DRAWING:
After a player plays a card, they draw a new one from the deck so they always have 5 cards.

END OF ROUND:
The player who could not play loses the round. The other player gets 1 point.
The player who won the round goes first in the next round.

DECK RULE:
If the deck runs out, a new shuffled deck is used automatically.

END OF GAME:
First player to reach 10 points wins the game.

NOTE:
This game is about timing. If you waste your high cards too early you can get stuck later with nothing to play.
